ChemKriti '26

ChemKriti is the annual flagship festival of the department of Chemical Engineering at NIT Durgapur. Organized by the Chemical Engineering Students’ Society (ChESS), it is a 5-day celebration of innovation and technical skill. The fest offers a unique platform for students to apply classroom concepts to real-world industrial challenges. From high-stakes technical events like ChemNiti and Codefest to creative talent hunts, ChemKriti is the ultimate union of logic, strategy, and engineering excellence.
